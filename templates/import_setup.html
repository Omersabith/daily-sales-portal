What the import reads

Sheet Required Columns Notes

SKUs `sku_code`, `sku_name`, `category`, `default_price`, `active` Only `active = yes` SKUs appear in promoter entry. `default_price` must be numeric.

Historical Sales `period_from`, `period_to`, `promoter_username`, `total_sales`, `notes`, `updated_at` Use this to backfill promoter totals for periods before the portal went live.
